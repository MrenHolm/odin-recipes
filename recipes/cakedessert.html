Ingredients

The Creation Phase (Directions)

  1. Prepare cake according to the package instructions and bake in a Bundt pan. Cool and invert onto cutting board.
  2. Cut a piece of cardboard large enough to accommodate two Bundt pans. Cover with aluminium foil.
  3. Carefully cut the cake in half and place on cardboard in "S" shape.
  4. Decorate with colored icing, candies, virgin blood, etc
